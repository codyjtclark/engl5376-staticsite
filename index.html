Who I am

I’m a recovering prescriptivist currently admitted at Texas Tech University. But old habits die hard—my bookcase is still littered with guides like Garner’s Modern English Usage and the Chicago Manual of Style.

By day I "technically" write for Google Cloud and have previously written for SendGrid, Informatica, and Honda R&D. By night I play in pit orchestras for musicals as well as larger ensembles including the Ballard Civic Orchestra, Washington Wind Symphony, and Northwest Clarinet Clarinet Choir.

In the rest of my free time, I like to add books to my TBR stacks, window shop for contra bass clarinets and arbitrarily omit serial commas.
